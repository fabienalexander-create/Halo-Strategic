Cookie Policy

Last updated: 22 July 2026
Short version: this site doesn't currently use tracking or advertising cookies. This page will be updated the moment that changes, rather than describing tools we don't actually use.

What a cookie is

A cookie is a small text file a website can ask your browser to store, typically used to remember you between visits or track behaviour across a site.

What we currently use

halostrategic.com does not set analytics, advertising, or behavioural tracking cookies. We haven't added a tool like Google Analytics, and there's no advertising pixel on this site.

Our contact form is processed through Netlify Forms, which may use a minimal functional cookie or token as part of its standard anti-spam and form-submission handling. This is not used to track you across the web, only to process the form itself. See Netlify's privacy documentation for detail on their infrastructure.

Fonts

This site loads typefaces from Google Fonts. This involves your browser making a request to Google's servers, which Google's own policies govern, but this method does not set a cookie in your browser under Google's current font-serving setup.

Controlling cookies

Most browsers let you view, block, or delete cookies through their settings. Since this site sets essentially none beyond what's described above, there's little to manage, but the option is there if you'd like to check for yourself.

Changes to this policy

If we add analytics, advertising, or any other cookie-setting tool in future, this page will be updated first, and the date at the top will change.
