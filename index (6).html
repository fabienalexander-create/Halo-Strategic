The Real Problem

Every department can be doing its job well, and the business still underperforms.

Marketing optimises for leads. Sales optimises for conversion. Operations optimises for delivery. Leadership wants profitable growth.

Every one of those can be working exactly as intended, and the business can still underperform, because no one is responsible for how they fit together. Not conflict. Just nobody watching the whole system.

Leadership Shouldn't Have to Guess

Why isn't growth translating into profit? Why does every department tell a different story about the same quarter? Which numbers can actually be trusted? What should happen next?

These aren't unusual questions. They're the ones most leadership teams are quietly living with. Halo exists to replace competing opinions with evidence, so those questions have answers, not just more opinions in the room.

The Gap

Most businesses receive detailed marketing reports every month. Very few receive clear, evidence-backed commercial recommendations.

A report describes what a campaign did. It doesn't say whether to scale it, hold it, or walk away, and answering that question isn't anyone's job by default. That's the gap Halo exists to close.

What Halo Is

Halo is a Commercial Growth Consultancy.

In practice, that means acting as a commercial operating partner: where a vendor sees a campaign, Halo sees a customer journey. Where a vendor sees a department, Halo sees one commercial system that either works together or quietly doesn't.

The work isn't to run your marketing. It's to tell you, with evidence, what's actually happening in your business, and what to do about it.

How Halo Works

Every engagement starts inside your actual systems, not a template.

Halo traces how a real customer moves through your business, end to end, before recommending anything.

From there, you get a clear signal on every part of the business that matters: scale this, hold that, fix this first, backed by evidence, on a cadence you can rely on.

The signal is not the decision. It's what makes the decision faster and better-informed.

The call is always made with judgement, by a person who knows your business, never handed off to a dashboard.

Why Halo Is Different

Halo doesn't sell campaigns. Halo doesn't sell reporting. Halo doesn't even sell decisions.

Halo sells commercial confidence: the difference between guessing and knowing, on the calls that actually matter: whether to scale, whether to hire, whether to stop, whether to change direction. Not by replacing judgement. By strengthening it with evidence a business can actually rely on.
